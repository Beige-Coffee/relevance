Mini-course · 3 modules

Parasitic Processing

After this course, students will be able to explain how the very cognitive machinery that makes human beings intelligently adaptive, heuristics, encoding specificity, confirmation bias, self-organizing dynamics, also makes them structurally vulnerable to self-reinforcing loops of self-deceptive, self-destructive behavior. They will understand why this vulnerability is not a moral failing but an architectural feature of intelligent agency, why willpower or propositional insight alone cannot break the spiral, and how the Buddhist Eightfold Path can be understood as a counteractive dynamical system. This concept is central to the series' diagnosis of why suffering persists despite awareness: it is endemic, not accidental. Parasitic processing connects Aristotle's akrasia, Buddhist dukkha, and Marc Lewis's reciprocal narrowing into a unified cognitive-scientific account.

Prerequisites: self-deception

Module 1

The Self-Organizing Loop: How Adaptive Heuristics Go Wrong

Trace how adaptive cognitive heuristics, representativeness, availability, confirmation bias, can combine into a self-reinforcing spiral of misperception and lost agency.

Exposition

Episode 13 · Buddhism and Parasitic Processing
“We called this in, when we published, 'Parasitic Processing'. It's not just about bad events. This is just one example. We get into all kinds of these spirals.”
The published term and its scope, it covers all self-reinforcing cognitive loops, not just depression.
Episode 38 · Agape and 4E Cognitive Science
“the very processes that make us intelligently adaptive also make us vulnerable to self-deceptive, self-destructive behavior”
The core paradox: the vulnerability is inseparable from the adaptive capacity.

Misconception branches

If: Parasitic processing is just another name for bad habits or laziness.
Then: In Vervaeke's account (with Ferraro), parasitic processing is self-organizing and self-reinforcing, it borrows the adaptive machinery of relevance realization and operates against the agent's interests. It is not a character defect but an architectural vulnerability of intelligent systems.
If: The perennial cognitive vulnerabilities Vervaeke describes are moral failures that more effort could overcome.
Then: Vervaeke is explicit: these are structural vulnerabilities built into the very machinery that makes intelligent agency possible, the representativeness heuristic, encoding specificity, confirmation bias are all adaptively indispensable. The issue is not weakness of character.

Walk through how Vervaeke's account of parasitic processing explains this spiral.

Module 2

Modal Confusion as a Driver of Parasitic Processing

Explain how attempting to satisfy being-mode needs through having-mode strategies generates the misfit that feeds parasitic processing.

Exposition

Episode 38 · Agape and 4E Cognitive Science
“We can address modal confusion by the cultivation of Sati.”
The positive formulation: sati counteracts modal confusion which drives the spiral.
Episode 13 · Buddhism and Parasitic Processing
“Marc proposes a model that he calls 'Reciprocal Narrowing'. So here's your Agent and here's the Arena (drawn on the board). And what happens is the drug use is associated with a particular Agent:Arena relationship.”
Marc Lewis's complementary model showing how parasitic processing and reciprocal narrowing interpenetrate.

Socratic seeds

How does modal confusion, trying to satisfy being-mode needs with having-mode strategies, generate the kind of frustration that feeds a cognitive spiral?
unmet being-need creates ongoing dissatisfactionincreased acquisition does not satisfyfrustration activates heuristic-driven spiraling
Marc Lewis's reciprocal narrowing describes addiction at the Agent-Arena level. How does it connect to parasitic processing at the cognitive level?
internal loop and external narrowing mutually reinforceloss of cognitive flexibility shrinks arenasame machinery involved in both
Vervaeke says dukkha, the Buddhist diagnosis, encompasses both parasitic processing and reciprocal narrowing. Why does this integration matter for understanding what enlightenment is trying to achieve?
enlightenment addresses both levelsanagoge as spiral upwardfull recovery requires internal and external opening

Misconception branches

If: Addiction is primarily a biochemical dependency problem.
Then: Lewis's model, which Vervaeke endorses, shows addiction is better understood as reciprocal narrowing of the Agent-Arena relationship through learned loss of agency, not primarily a biochemical lock. Vietnam veterans' spontaneous cessation upon returning home illustrates this.

Check for understanding

Module 3

The Eightfold Path as Counteractive Dynamical System

Explain why only a counteractive dynamical system, not a single-point intervention, can address parasitic processing, and how the Eightfold Path exemplifies this.

Exposition

Episode 13 · Buddhism and Parasitic Processing
“The Eightfold Path is a counteractive dynamical system that counteracts parasitic processing and does reciprocal opening, beyond the ego self and beyond the everyday world”
The central therapeutic claim, a coordinated multi-level system rather than single-point intervention.

Socratic seeds

Vervaeke says that if you try to intervene at a single point in a complex self-organizing system, the system just reorganizes around your intervention. Why does this mean a multi-level counteractive system is required?
complex systems have emergent propertiessingle intervention gets absorbedneed simultaneous coordinated intervention at all levels
The Eightfold Path addresses cognition (right understanding, right thought), character (right speech, action, livelihood), and consciousness (right mindfulness, concentration). Why does a response to parasitic processing need to work at all three levels?
process operates at all three levels simultaneouslycharacter without consciousness leaves blind spotscognition without character is akratic
Vervaeke says the Eightfold Path is supposed to roll itself, to be self-organizing. What does that mean, and why is that feature essential for it to work against parasitic processing?
counterforce must match self-organizing character of parasitic processingpractices mutually support each othersystem builds momentum through interdependence

Misconception branches

If: Understanding that you are in a self-destructive pattern is sufficient to begin escaping it.
Then: Vervaeke is clear that propositional insight, knowing about the spiral, does not break it, because the process is self-organizing and operates below deliberate control. A counteractive dynamical system working simultaneously at cognitive, character, and consciousness levels is required.

Check for understanding

In what sense is the Eightfold Path a scientific rather than merely religious response to suffering, in Vervaeke's account? How does the concept of counteractive dynamical systems allow us to evaluate it on cognitive-scientific grounds?
matches complex system with complex counterforceempirically testable modelwisdom tradition articulating cognitive insight not merely dogma
